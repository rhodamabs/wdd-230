Target Audience

The site is targetting business owners in the city, the local authorities and potential investors who want to start new businesses in the city.

Personas

Persona #1: An investor interested in establishing a business in the City. Want to know the legal procedures required to do business in the city. This person is going access the site with phone, tablet or computer.

Persona #2: A CEO of a company in the city interested in upcoming news and trade fairs This person is going to use this website via desktop, tablet or phone.

Scenarios

  • Possible scenarios for site visits.
  • A bussiness owner interested in news updates like government annual budget.
  • A business owner interested in upcoming events like trade fairs.
  • Investors interested in business potentials in the city.
  • Trade organisations interested in business in the city for event planning purposes.
  • Government Authorities interested in business running in the city.
