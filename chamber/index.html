News Release

Tax Amnesty Reminder

The Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA) hereby urges its valued clients to take full advantage of the tax amnesty to regularise their tax affairs. The tax amnesty is in respect of any non-compliance which occurred during the period beginning 1 February 2009 to 30 September 2014 (the amnesty period). Clients who wish to benefit from the tax amnesty are required to submit applications on Form No. TAO1, which is obtainable from the ZIMRA website (www.zimra.co.zw), to the Commissioner General of ZIMRA within the period 1 October 2014 to 31 March 2015.


There are, therefore, less than two months left before ZIMRA closes the acceptance of applications for the tax amnesty. There will be no extension to the period within which applications must be submitted.
